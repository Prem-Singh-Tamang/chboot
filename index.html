Canadian Golden Retrievers are essentially the same as American Goldens, with minimal variation. These Goldens have a long golden coat that can vary slightly in the shade. They stand at the same height as adult American Golden Retrievers, with males being 23-24 inches tall and females being 21.5-22.5 inches tall.

An English cream golden retriever is a golden retriever descended from an English line with a light-colored coat. This breed is not officially recognized by any kennel club, which means that many breeders may claim to offer English cream golden retrievers for sale without much oversight.

Retriever's Behaviour

Goldens are outgoing, trustworthy, and eager-to-please family dogs, and relatively easy to train. They take a joyous and playful approach to life and maintain this puppyish behavior into adulthood. These energetic, powerful gundogs enjoy outdoor play. The golden retriever is even-tempered, intelligent and affectionate. Golden retrievers are playful, yet gentle with children, and they tend to get along well with other pets and strangers. These dogs are eager to please, which probably explains why they respond so well to obedience training and are such popular service dogs. They also like to work, whether it involves hunting birds or fetching their guardian's slippers.. Golden retrievers are not often barkers, and they lack guard instincts, so do not count on them to make good watchdogs. However, some golden retrievers will let you know when strangers are approaching
